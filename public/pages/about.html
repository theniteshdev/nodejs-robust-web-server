About — How to Learn In Depth

A concise guide and study plan for gaining deep understanding of technical topics.

Philosophy

Deep learning is deliberate: focus on fundamentals, apply knowledge, and iterate with feedback.

Core principles

  • Start with concepts, not tools.
  • Build small projects that force understanding.
  • Teach or write about what you learn.
  • Repeat spaced practice and review.

30-day study plan (example)

  1. Week 1: fundamentals and core concepts.
  2. Week 2: small project and read source material.
  3. Week 3: refactor, optimize, and write notes.
  4. Week 4: teach, document, and connect concepts.
